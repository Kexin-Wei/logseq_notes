# 07 — pi_0: A VLA Flow Model for General Robot Control

**Year:** 2024 | **Venue:** Physical Intelligence | **Tier:** S | **Status:** ( ) unread
**Link:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.24164

---

## Summary

pi_0 is a ~2B-parameter VLA from Physical Intelligence that replaces the discrete action-token head of RT-2 with a **flow-matching continuous-action head** on top of a PaliGemma-based VLM backbone, enabling **50 Hz real-time control** across diverse manipulation tasks. It is trained on a large cross-embodiment dataset and demonstrates broad generalization. The paper matters for VLA writing because it marks the clear transition from token-based VLAs (RT-2, OpenVLA) to continuous-flow VLAs — a distinction that the action-tokenization taxonomy [@zhong2025vla] uses as a primary axis.

**Taxonomy slot:** action representation = raw action via flow matching; 50 Hz inference.
