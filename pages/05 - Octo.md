# 05 — Octo

Lightweight open-source generalist robot policy combining a transformer backbone with a diffusion action head, trained on Open X-Embodiment. Worth *knowing exists* as an open alternative to OpenVLA, but not worth deep reading — the method is an assembly of existing parts (transformer VLA + diffusion head) rather than a new idea.

**One-liner:** > Octo [@octo2024] is an open-source generalist robot policy pairing a transformer backbone with a diffusion action head, trained on the Open X-Embodiment dataset.
