- ## S (Skim) Template
  Copy this when starting notes for a **skim-tier paper** (~30-minute read). Filled-in reference: `07 - pi_0.md` (under `Embodied AI/Paper Notes/`).
- ### Frontmatter
  ```yaml
  paper_number: NN
  title: "..."
  year: YYYY
  venue: ...
  section: S1 | S2 | ...
  tier: S
  status: unread | skimmed
  link: https://...
  bibtex_key: authorYYYYfirstword
  primary_theme: ...
  themes: [<primary>]
  ```
- ### Immediately after frontmatter
  `tags:: tier/S, <theme>` (add `survey` if it is one)
- ### Title line
  `# NN — <Paper Title>`
- ## Summary
  _~100-word paragraph: what the paper does + one headline result + why it matters for your theme. One paragraph, no sub-headings._
- **Taxonomy slot:** _where this paper sits on its theme's organizing axis_
  (e.g., VLA papers: "Action representation: raw action + flow matching, 50 Hz")
- ## Connections
	- 1-2 bullets — how this paper extends / complements another
- ## One-liner
  > Drop-in sentence for related-work citation.
