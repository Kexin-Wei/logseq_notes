- Find a Good Stock
	- 3+1 Profit Index
		- Profitability
			- $\text{Net Profit Margin}=\text{Net Profit} / \text{Revenue}$
			- higher better
		- Finance Health
			- $\text{Current Ratio} = \text{Current Assets} / \text{Current Liabilities}$
			- better >1.5, at least > 1
		- Price Level
			- $\text{Price to Earn Ratio}=\text{Share Price}/\text{}$
